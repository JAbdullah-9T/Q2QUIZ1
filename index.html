Bicol (Luzon) People in Bicol are called Bicolano. They practice agriculture and religious beliefs such as practicing Catholic. Bicol Express, Laing, Pili nuts
Tarlac (Luzon) Tarlac has a mixture of 4 ethnic groups, which are the Ilocanos, Pangasinans, Kapampangans, and the Tagalogs. Because of this, it makes Tarlac one of the most multi-cultural provinces in the region. They also practice agriculture with a variety of crops like rice, corn, and sugarcane. Bagis, Tupig
Negros Oriental (Visayas) Negros' culture is rich and diverse because of it's history. One example is the Indigenous people/tribes who inhabited the land like the Negritos and Austronesians. Another example is that during the time when the Spaniards were around, the left behind structures like the Cathedral. Inato Pecho, Budbod Kabog, Barquiron
Zamboanga (Mindanao) Zamboanga is known for their their colorful culture and tradition like their festivals. It is also a popular place because of how naturally beautiful the place is, making it a perfect tourist place. Paella, Callos, Bacalao
